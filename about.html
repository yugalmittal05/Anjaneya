About Anjaneya College

A decade of empowering students in Delhi with professional education, industry exposure, and the confidence to build thriving careers.

Our Story

Rooted in . Committed to Quality Education.

Anjaneya College of Higher Education was founded in 2014 with a clear mission: to make quality professional education accessible to every aspiring student in our region. Starting with a handful of courses and a single campus, we have grown into one of the most trusted educational institutions in our region — offering 40+ programmes across teacher training, management, technology, commerce, and vocational streams.

Over the last decade, more than 4,500 students have walked through our doors and emerged as confident professionals. Our graduates teach in schools , lead departments in corporations, run their own businesses, and serve in government roles. Their success is the truest measure of our work.

We believe that education is not just about earning a certificate — it is about building capability, character, and the confidence to pursue your ambitions.

Our Journey

10 Years of Growing Together

  • 2014 — Founded

    Anjaneya College of Higher Education opened its doors in Delhi with 3 courses and an initial batch of 80 students.

  • 2016 — First Placement Drive

    Launched our dedicated placement cell. 94% of the inaugural batch secured employment or higher education admissions within 6 months.

  • 2018 — 1,000 Students Milestone

    Crossed 1,000 enrolled students. Expanded course offerings to 20+ programmes across teacher training, management, and technology.

  • 2020 — Online Learning Integration

    Introduced hybrid and online learning options during the pandemic — ensuring zero disruption to students' academic timelines.

  • 2022 — Delhi Education Award

    Recognised as "Best Professional Institute" at the Delhi Education Awards for consistent placement and academic outcomes.

  • 2024 — 4,500+ Graduates & 40+ Courses

    Today, Anjaneya College is home to 40+ programmes, 4,500+ alumni, and a placement network spanning 200+ partner companies.
