Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission?
Generally: 10th & 12th marksheets, graduation certificates (for PG courses), passport-size photographs, Aadhaar card or government ID, and domicile/caste certificate if applicable. Your counsellor will share the exact checklist based on the course you apply for.
Does Anjaneya College offer EMI or instalment payment options?
Yes. We offer easy instalment plans for most courses. The fee can typically be paid in 2–4 instalments over the course duration. Discuss the plan with your counsellor at the time of enrolment.
Are the certificates issued by Anjaneya College recognised?
All degree programmes are affiliated with recognised universities and are UGC-approved. Diploma and certificate programmes are issued by the institute and carry industry recognition. Specific affiliation details per course can be shared by your counsellor.
Is there a separate batch for working professionals?
Yes. We run morning batches (7:30 AM – 10:00 AM), evening batches (6:00 PM – 8:30 PM), and weekend batches (Saturday & Sunday) specifically for working professionals. Batch schedules vary by course.
What is your placement process like?
Our dedicated placement cell begins preparing students 3 months before course completion. This includes resume building, mock interviews, LinkedIn profile setup, and company presentations. We have 200+ partner companies NCR actively hiring from our pool.
